Beaconhouse School System (BSS) Main Campus Multan

History

The Beaconhouse School System (BSS) was established in 1975, beginning as a small nursery school. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest private school networks in Pakistan. The Main Campus in Multan is a significant part of this network, offering a comprehensive education to students across various age groups.

Total Number of Students Enrolled

The campus accommodates hundreds and thousands of students and staff.

Sections

Early Years

The Early Years section caters to the foundational education of children, focusing on cognitive development, social skills, and basic literacy and numeracy.

Primary Section

The Primary Section builds on the foundational skills acquired in Early Years, offering a broad curriculum that includes subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and more.

Boys Section

The Boys Section provides education tailored to male students, offering both academic and co-curricular activities to ensure holistic development.

Girls Section

The Girls Section is dedicated to female students, with a focus on both academics and personal development through various co-curricular activities.

Streams Offered

  • Matriculation (Matric)
  • O-Levels (Cambridge System)
  • A-Levels

Educational Board

Matriculation: Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE) Multan

O-Levels and A-Levels: Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE)
